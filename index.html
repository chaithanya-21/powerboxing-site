The Format

48 Minutes.
Perfectly Structured.

Every session follows the same format. Zero guesswork. You push harder every time you come back.

W
Warm-Up · 5 min
Get Moving
Dynamic warm-up with footwork and mobility drills. Heart rate climbs, the music kicks in, and you're ready to go.
1
Round 1 · 5 min
Jabs & Straights
Build rhythm with crisp jab-cross combinations synced to the beat. The bag sensors start scoring every punch.
2
Round 2 · 5 min
Power Hooks
Add hooks to your combos. Rotate through the hips, feel the power transfer. Music drops — your combo gets faster.
3
Round 3 · 5 min
Core Strength Block
Off the bag for bodyweight strength work — squats, planks, burpees. Lungs burning, but the beat keeps you going.
4–6
Rounds 4–6 · 15 min
Full Combination Fire
The heart of the session. Full 6-punch combos, peak-tempo music, 20 people going all out. This is where the results happen.
7
Final Round · 5 min
Leave It All Here
Final round, all out. Last chance to push your score. The room erupts. You leave nothing behind.
C
Cool-Down · 5 min
Breathe & Stretch
Full body stretch. Your score is revealed. 48 minutes done — and you'll be back for more.
